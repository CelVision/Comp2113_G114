Level 10: Endless Tower Defense Mode

-wave amount: infinite waves (endless mode)

Map Layout:
- Space characters (' ') = Enemy patrol routes (passages through the map)
- Dot characters ('.') = Non-path areas, suitable for tower placement
- 'M' surrounded by '#' = 3x3 home base
- Row 2, 3, 4 left side: Only entry point for all enemies (space corridor on the left edge)

Description: This is an endless survival mode where waves continue indefinitely. The first 5 waves are designed to gradually increase in difficulty with more enemies and stronger types. All enemies enter through the left passage (row 2, 3, 4 left side). Starting from wave 6, enemy spawn count remains constant, but enemy HP follows a logistic growth formula dy/dx = k*y*(A - y) to approach the upper limit A. Every 10 waves (integer multiples), a dragon boss wave appears.

Logistic Growth Parameters:
- Initial HP multiplier: 1.0 (at wave 6)
- A (upper limit): 10000 (HP cap)
- k (growth rate): 0.1
- HP at wave n: HP = A / (1 + (A/HP_base - 1) * exp(-k * (n - 6)))

Where HP_base is the original HP from MobData.txt, scaled by wave.

For waves 1-5: Standard progression.
For waves 6+: Fixed spawn count, HP scaling.

Dragon Boss Waves: Every 10th wave (10, 20, 30, etc.), the Dragon Boss appears in three phases. The Dragon Boss starts as Type 1, transitions to Type 2 after losing a portion of HP, then to Type 3. Each phase's HP is scaled using the logistic growth formula, growing with wave number.

- Phase 1: Dragon Type 1 (HP scaled)
- Phase 2: Dragon Type 2 (HP scaled, after Phase 1 HP is depleted)
- Phase 3: Dragon Type 3 (HP scaled, after Phase 2 HP is depleted)

Scaling: For wave n (10,20,30,...), HP_multiplier = A / (1 + (A/HP_base - 1) * exp(-k * (n/10 - 1))), where HP_base is the original HP for each type.
