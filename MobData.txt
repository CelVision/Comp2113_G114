=== ENEMY DIFFICULTY LEVELS ===

Tier 1 (Easy): Pigman, Houndling
- Low hp, basic enemies for tutorial levels

Tier 2 (Medium): Werewolf, Mini Mammon, Birdman, Spiderman
- Moderate hp and speed, introduce armor and special abilities

Tier 3 (Hard): Armored Mammon, Batman, Pigman Berserker, Alpha Wolf
- High hp, armor, or special effects
- Requires tower combinations to defeat

Tier 4 (Boss): All Dragon types
- Extremely high hp and armor
- Special attack patterns
- Late game challenge

=== RECOMMENDED COUNTER TOWERS ===

Pigman → Arrow Tower (2 shots), Laser Tower
Houndling → Laser Tower, Frost Tower
Werewolf → Frost Tower (to slow), Hell Tower
Mini Mammon → Earthquake Tower, Hell Tower
Armored Mammon → Hell Tower (percentage), Armor Penetration Tower
Birdman → Laser Tower, Earthquake Tower
Batman → Frost Tower (to catch), Laser Tower
Pigman Berserker → Frost Tower (to prevent speedup), Armor Penetration Tower
Spiderman → Multiple towers (to overcome slowdown)
Alpha Wolf → Multiple towers (for adds)
Dragon Type 1 → Earthquake Tower, Multiple towers
Dragon Type 2 → Hell Tower, Armor Penetration Tower
Dragon Type 3 → Laser Tower, Frost Tower
